About me

Hello my name is Trishanna Lennon and I am a first year graphic design student at Algonquin College. I have been honing my skills through personal project and continuing building my strong foundation in both traditional and digital art. While I am working for a agency I would want to have my own buisness where I can create and sell high quality art prints.
